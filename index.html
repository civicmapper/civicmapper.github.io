civicmapper

civicmapper is a geographic information services company focused on building open architecture solutions for open data for the greater good

Open by default. Our work is guided by open data principles and concepts. We work to make geodata accessible and transparent from project inception to implementation.

Data as a living product. Data is not just a deliverable - it is an evolving element of your organization. We work with you to structure existing processes to support maintenance of data so that it grows and supports your mission.

Platform integration. We use the platforms for geographic data collection, collaboration, and dissemination tools that are right for your needs now, with an eye towards future aspirations.

Agile and iterative development. Whether the goal is to building a simple map or a complex geospatial database, new information gleaned from the development process can necessitate a change in approach or scope. We employ an agile and iterative spiral approach to development, testing early and breaking often, in order to optimize the solution early.
