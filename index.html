Approach

Our approach to our work is guided by four principles:

  • Open by default.

    We work to make geodata accessible and transparent from project inception to implementation, and look to open data and open software concepts as a model. This holistic, open-architecture approach to GIS ensures that the work we do is built upon existing standards and integrates with existing workflows.

  • Data as a living product.

    Data is not just a deliverable - it is an evolving and critical element of your organization. We work with you to structure existing processes to support the easy maintenance of data so that the data grows and supports your mission, without distracting from it.

  • Evolving and integrated platforms.

    When it comes to geographic information systems, there are a lot of software and hardware options out there to choose from. We use the platform(s) for geographic data collection, collaboration, and dissemination that are matched for your organization's needs now, with an eye towards the future.

  • Agile and iterative development.

    Whether the goal is to build a simple map or a complex geospatial database, new information gleaned from the development process can necessitate a change in approach or scope. We employ an agile and iterative spiral approach to both data collection and software development in order to reach an optimal solution.
