CIVICMAPPER

We are a team of experts in the application of geographic information systems (GIS) from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.

Our mission is to enable and empower our clients to use the geospatial data they depend on to better support their missions. Our work is guided by four principles:

Open by default.

We work to make geodata accessible and transparent from project inception to implementation, and look to open data and open software concepts as a model. This holistic, open-architecture approach to GIS ensures that the work we do is built upon existing standards, integrates with existing workflows, and ultimately, will endure.

Data as a living product.

Data is not just a deliverable - it is an evolving and critical element of your organization. We work with you to structure existing processes to support the easy maintenance of data so that the data grows and supports your mission, without distracting from it.

Evolving and integrated platforms.

When it comes to geographic information systems, there are a lot of software and hardware options out there to choose from. We use the platform(s) for geographic data collection, collaboration, and dissemination that are matched for your organization's needs now, with an eye towards the future.

Agile and iterative development.

Whether the goal is to build a simple map or a complex geospatial database, new information gleaned from the development process can necessitate a change in approach or scope. We employ an agile and iterative spiral approach to data collection, data maintenance, and software development in order to reach the optimal solution.
